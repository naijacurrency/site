About The Black Market Tracker

Purpose
The app is a response to the need of lots of Nigerians all over the world to know the current real (black market)exchange rates.

The app provides live exchange rates for 4 major currencies popular in Nigeria and also a calculator to use these exchanges

Exchange rates
The App collects data from popular exchange providers to give you actual rates you will find in the market when yu attempt to carry out your exchange.The App obtains data from Binance and Kraken exchanges

You can use the rates provided as a guide but you will never find any rates that will go lower than this. However, you may find exhangers will go a bit higher than these rates because their exchange commissions have been included.

Supported Currencies
The currencies supported are CAD, GBP, NGN, USD and EURO. If you would like to see more currencies, kindly send us an email at hello@naijacurrencyconverter.com.ng
