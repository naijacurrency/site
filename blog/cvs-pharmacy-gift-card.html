About gift cards

A gift card is like a prepaid card you can use instead of cash to shop. It’s super handy and makes a great gift because it gives the recipient the freedom to choose what they want. You basically load a set amount of money onto the card, and they can use it at a specific store or, in some cases, anywhere that accepts it.

There are two main types of gift cards: physical and digital. Physical ones are those plastic cards that look like credit cards, often branded with the store’s logo. Digital gift cards are sent via email or text, which makes them perfect if you’re in a hurry or if you just want something that’s quick and easy.

You might see gift cards for a single retailer—like a coffee shop or clothing store—or ones that work across multiple places, thanks to networks like Visa or Mastercard. Keep in mind, some gift cards might have expiration dates or fees, so it’s good to check the details.

For the person receiving the gift card, it’s like giving them a mini shopping spree. They get to pick out exactly what they want, which can be way better than guessing their favorite color or size. For the person giving the card, it takes away the stress of finding the perfect gift. Overall, gift cards are a win-win: thoughtful, flexible, and super convenient.

How to Use this Pharmacy Gift Card Online

Using a CVS Pharmacy gift card online is easy. Follow these steps:

  1. Visit the official CVS Pharmacy website.
  2. Select the items you want to purchase and add them to your cart.
  3. During checkout, choose "Gift Card" as your payment method.
  4. Enter your CVS gift card number and PIN.
  5. The balance on your card will be applied to your order total.

Unfortunately, CVS gift cards can only be used on CVS's U.S. site and cannot be used to pay for orders shipping outside the U.S. However, if you're in Nigeria, you can still use them to buy products for friends or family in the U.S., or you can use third-party services that allow for international shopping.
