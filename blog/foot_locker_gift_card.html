What is a Foot Locker Gift Card?

A Foot Locker gift card is a prepaid card that can be used to purchase products from any Foot Locker store or online at FootLocker.com. Available in physical and digital (eGift) formats, these cards make shopping easy and flexible. Simply load a specific amount onto the card and use it as a payment method for your favorite sneakers or apparel.

How to Redeem a Foot Locker Gift Card

Redeeming a Foot Locker gift card is simple and can be done both in-store and online.

In-Store Redemption:

  1. Visit a Foot Locker store and select the items you want to purchase.
  2. Present your gift card at checkout.
  3. The cashier will scan the card, and the balance will be applied to your purchase.

Online Redemption:

  1. Visit FootLocker.com and add items to your cart.
  2. At checkout, select "Gift Card" as your payment method.
  3. Enter the card number and PIN, then click "Apply."
  4. The gift card balance will be deducted from your total.
